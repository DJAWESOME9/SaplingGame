🌱 Welcome to Sapling

You're growing a tree where every branch is both the map and the upgrade screen. Sap flows from the leaves down through the trunk to a Core — what you spend to grow more.

  • 🌿Grow leaves on the trunk to produce sap. Click the core to start.
  • ⚡Amplifiers multiply any flow passing through them — branch deep or wide.
  • 🧪Refineries turn sap into resin; Capacitors store flow and burst it back boosted.
  • 🪓Prune branches for amber, which buys advancements in the Roots tree (click the core to see it).
  • 🍯Gold leaves appear on leaves over time — click to harvest a sap windfall. Rare big gold leaves trigger a 30-second ×3 Bloom.
  • 🍃Cut the tree when it's grown to bank rings — eternal upgrades and new species in the Grove.

Tip: tap the 🔓 lock on any node's panel to protect it from accidental upgrades or pruning. Drag to pan, pinch or scroll to zoom.
